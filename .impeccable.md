## Design Context

### Users
Barış Şev's personal portfolio — primary audience is potential clients and collaborators in product, design, and AI. Viewed on desktop during deliberate browsing (not casual scrolling). The visitor is evaluating whether to work with Barış.

### Brand Personality
Precise, unhurried, confident. Three words: **editorial · spacious · considered**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light theme. Clean editorial with generous whitespace. No decoration for its own sake — every element earns its place. Anti-references: busy agency portfolios, dashboard-heavy layouts, anything with glowing gradients or AI clichés. References: typographic editorial sites, Apple product pages (restraint, not spectacle).

### Design System
- **Fonts**: Sora (`--display`) for headings, Figtree (`--body`) for body
- **Colors**: OKLCH throughout. Background: `oklch(97.4% 0.006 238)`. Text: `oklch(9% 0.012 245)`. Cards: white `oklch(1 0 0)`.
- **Spacing tokens**: `--sp-xxs` 4px · `--sp-xs` 8px · `--sp-sm` 12px · `--sp-md` 17px · `--sp-lg` 24px · `--sp-xl` 32px · `--sp-xxl` 48px · `--sp-section` 80px
- **Radius tokens**: `--r-xs` 5px · `--r-sm` 8px · `--r-md` 11px · `--r-lg` 18px · `--r-pill` 9999px · `--r-full` 50%
- **Elevation**: Flat. No drop shadows on cards. Hairline border: `1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`.

### Design Principles
1. Space is content — whitespace is never wasted.
2. One focal point per section — don't compete with yourself.
3. Typography does the heavy lifting — no icon grids, no decorative cards.
4. Motion is purposeful — animate to reveal, not to impress.
5. System-consistent — every new element must feel like it was always there.
